lasagna

Description

To make a classic lasagna, start by preparing the meat sauce. Sauté diced onions and garlic in olive oil until softened, then add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in tomato paste, canned tomatoes, and a mix of Italian seasonings like basil, oregano, and thyme. Let the sauce simmer for about 30 minutes to develop the flavors. While the sauce cooks, boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions and set them aside. For the cheese layer, mix ricotta cheese with an egg, grated Parmesan, chopped parsley, and a pinch of nutmeg.

To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Add a layer of cooked noodles, then spread a portion of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by more meat sauce, and a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese. Repeat these layers until you reach the top of the dish, finishing with a generous layer of mozzarella. Cover the dish with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for about 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes until the top is golden and bubbly. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before serving to allow it to set.

Ingredients

  • Meat Sauce:
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 onion, diced
    • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 pound ground beef
    • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    • 1 can (28 ounces) tomatoes (crushed or diced)
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lasagna Noodles:
    • 12-16 lasagna noodles (depending on the size of your baking dish)
  • Cheese Layer:
    • 2 cups ricotta cheese
    • 1 large egg
    • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (or 1 tablespoon dried parsley)
    • A pinch of nutmeg
  • Additional Ingredients:
    • 2-3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • For Baking:
    • Aluminum foil
